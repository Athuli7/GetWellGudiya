About

The tragic accident that took place in Varappuzha on 2nd January between a bus and a car took away the lives of two students Jijisha and Akshay of School of Engineering, CUSAT. The accident also left the 3 other friends, Athulya, Athira and Gudiya travelling in the same car battling for their life. All the three girls are undergoing intensive treatment at Aster Medicity, Cochin and the medical bills are piling up to lakhs.

Out of the three, Gudiya S Lal hails from a not so strong financial background and her parents are finding it extremely hard to provide her with the best medical care. She has already underwent multiple surgeries including a major one for spine which alone costs more than Rs 5 lakhs. As per the doctors' advice she has to spend the another four weeks atleast in the hospital for recovery. Both her parents are tailors and are trying their best to come up with the huge amount for treatment.

The best we can do is to pray for their well being and contribute as much as we possibly can for her hospital bills.We have set up a fund raising campaign for Gudiya. The details can be found below. Every contribution, small or large, is greatly welcomed ! Let's bring her back to class.
